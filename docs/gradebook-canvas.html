Format Canvas Gradebook

This feature creates a CSV formatted for upload to Faculty Center. It requires a completed exported (downloaded) Canvas gradebook CSV file.

Who Can use Format Canvas Gradebook

  • Root Account Administrator
  • Sub-Account Administrator
  • Support Consultant
  • Teachers and other course managers who normally have access to the People tool

How to Use Format Canvas Gradebook

  1. Click the Format Canvas Gradebook button on landing page.
  2. Follow the instructions from the Requirement section on the “Upload your CSV File” page.
  3. Drag and drop, or click the link to upload your exported gradebook CSV file.
  4. The tool will convert your file to a Faculty Center compatible format and download it back to your computer.
